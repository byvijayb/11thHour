Shipping Policy

11th Hour is a fully digital product. There are no physical goods, no deliveries, and no shipping of any kind.

Cancellation & Refunds

All offerings made on 11th Hour are voluntary donations. Because there is no product being purchased in exchange, there is no cancellation flow.

However, if you made an offering by mistake, or if a technical error caused a duplicate charge, we will refund you in full. No questions asked.

To request a refund, email us at 11thhour.space@gmail.com within 7 days of the transaction with your transaction ID. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

We do not offer partial refunds. If you are eligible, the full amount will be returned.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here is exactly what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect: When you use 11th Hour, we store only the following — the emotional state you selected, the letter ID shown to you (to prevent repeats), and if you make an offering, the transaction amount. We do not collect your name from the input field — it is used only to personalise your letter and is never stored.

What we do not collect: We do not collect your email, phone number, location, device ID, or any identifying information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, when contacting us for a refund).

Cookies and storage: We use browser localStorage only to track which letters you have already seen, so we do not repeat them. This data lives only on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Third parties: We use Supabase for anonymous session data and Razorpay for payment processing. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions. We do not sell, share, or rent any data to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention: Anonymous session data is retained for analytics purposes. If you contact us via email, your email address is retained only for as long as needed to resolve your query.

Questions about privacy? Email 11thhour.space@gmail.com.
